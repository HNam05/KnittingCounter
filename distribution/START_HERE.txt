Knitting Counter Overlay
========================

How to start:

1. Extract this ZIP file first.
2. Open the extracted folder.
3. Double-click "Knitting Counter Overlay.exe".

If Windows shows a blue warning:

1. Click "More info".
2. Click "Run anyway".

Why the warning appears:

This is a small personal app and the file is not code-signed yet. The app works offline and stores its knitting projects locally on this computer.

How to close the app:

- In compact mode, click the small X button in the top-right corner.
- In manager mode, click "Close app".
